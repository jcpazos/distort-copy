New Account

Step 1. Twitter Information

A Twistor account is linked to a Twitter account in your browser session. Twistor will post on Twitter using that account. You may sign out from Twitter and re-login as a different account.

Connect

Step 2. Streaming App

To stream hashtags for incoming messages, Twistor relies on Twitter application credentials associated with your account. Twistor can create an application and tokens for you automatically. There must be a mobile phone number associated with the account. (visit apps site).

Step 3. Setup Initial Keys

Each Twistor account maintains a set of keys. If you have keys from a previous installation already, you may use them and import them here. Otherwise, the extension will automatically generate random set for you.

Last step. Review Changes

Review your chosen settings, and hit 'Finish' to complete the account creation.
